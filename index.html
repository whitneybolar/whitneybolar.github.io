Whitney Bolar

Pronouns: they/them/their

I am a technical communicator from Eastern Washington University, 20 miles west from Spokane, Washington. Growing up in the Seattle region, I realized that I enjoyed explaining complicated subjects to people in order to help them understand other perspectives. I also have an interest in the multiple ways people communicate, and how visuals influence others. As a full time student, I enjoy working on projects that expect me to work outside the box to impress my clients and mentors. I anticipate to graduate in June 2020 with a major in technical communications and a minor in visual communication design and a certificate in user experience.
